This weekend I spent time re-organizing our stereo setup, and it's given me a sense of ownership over our space again. I've really missed listening to my records and tapes. Saturday it was really nice and sunny, so I decided to stop at Psychic Records on my way back from running errands, something I haven't done since living in NYC. I picked up Moon Beams by The Bill Evans Trio and found a 3 disc set of Ravel. When I asked how much the Ravel was, the clerk said "$5 for that one. Did you take a look? To be honest, I don't pay much attention to the classical stuff." I told him I'd be willing to risk it for $5. I'm very happy about it, some of my all time favorite piano pieces are in there. People under appreciate classical music. Nothing makes my soul feel like springtime like Ravel.
