Hi, I'm Jake and I'm from Salt Lake City UT currently living in Brooklyn NY. I use code to design cool projects and to experiment with new ideas. In my free time I love to read, write, and fall down rabbit holes, usually related to history and art.

This site is my attempt at reclaiming a corner of the internet as a place to share and connect in a way that's meaningful and breaks free from the exploitative algorithms subjected on us by Big Tech. Feel free to surf around and if you feel so inclined, leave a note in my guest book, or shoot me an email.

Thanks for stopping by!
