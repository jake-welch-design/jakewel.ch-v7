bio.md (2.66kB)

Hi! I'm Jake and I'm from Salt Lake City, UT currently living in Brooklyn, NY. I use code to design cool projects and to experiment with new ideas. In my free time I love to read, write, and fall down research and archiving rabbit holes, usually related to history, art, and the internet.

This site is my attempt at reclaiming a corner of the internet as a place to share and connect in a way that's meaningful and breaks free from the exploitative algorithms imposed on us by Big Tech. If you're at all interested in having your own personal site, I've written a beginner-friendly guide on how to do so for free!

Feel free to surf around, and if you feel so inclined, leave a note in my guest book or shoot me an email.

Thanks for visiting!
